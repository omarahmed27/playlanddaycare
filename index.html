Daily Rhythm

A Day in the Life
at Playland.

Every day follows a warm, predictable rhythm so children feel safe, engaged, and ready to learn.

7:00 AM
🌅
Arrival & Free Play
Children arrive and settle in with open-ended free play to ease into the day.
8:30 AM
🔵
Circle Time
Songs, stories, calendar, and group conversation to build language and social skills.
9:30 AM
🎨
Arts & Crafts / Science
Hands-on creative and science exploration activities to spark curiosity.
11:00 AM
🌳
Outdoor Play
Fresh air, gross motor play, and nature exploration in a safe outdoor space.
12:00 PM
🍽️
Lunch
A calm, social meal time. Children are encouraged to try new foods and practice manners.
1:00 PM
😴
Nap Time / Quiet Time
Rest and recharge. Older children have quiet independent play or books.
3:00 PM
🎵
Music & Movement / Games
Energetic group play, music, dancing, and structured games.
4:30 PM
📖
Story Time & Wind Down
A calming end to the activity day with stories and gentle group activities.
5:00 PM
🏠
Pick-up & Free Play
Open play as families arrive. Open until 6:00 PM.

Questions.

Yes — Playland Day Care is a fully licensed home daycare in San Jose, CA. Lubna is also First Aid and CPR certified, providing a safe and regulated environment for your child.
